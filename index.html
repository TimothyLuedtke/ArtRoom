Consult

Consultation
Detailed Overview Of A Property
Time: 1.5-2 hrs

A consultation starts every project to help identify the work parameters. It is a great opportunity to ask questions and get feedback on your home.

We speak with you about your specific wants/needs, take photographs and dimensions of the staging areas, and create a checklist of actionable items to complete the stage.

We will also provide you with a detailed overview of the project proposal and a quote for the work.
