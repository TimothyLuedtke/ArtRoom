Process

Consult

Consultation

Our first step is to meet with you to discuss your goals and expectations for your home sale. We'll discuss your timeline, budget, and any other concerns you may have. We'll also discuss the current market and how we can help you achieve your goals.

Prep

Preperation

Most of the items on this checklist will come directly from our earlier conversations.

There are steps that are always necessary before selling including decluttering and cleaning.

You may choose to take on tasks yourself or ask us to use our vast network of industry professionals. From cleaning to painting, we're happy to help.

Stage

Staging

Staging is the process of preparing your home for sale. It's a combination of decluttering, cleaning, and decorating.

Staging is a great way to make your home stand out from the competition and help you achieve your goals.

Our team will work with you to create a plan that fits your budget and timeline.
